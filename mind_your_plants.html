mind your plants

wordpress // theme customization // blog // photography

Mind Your Plants is a blog I created a couple of years ago - my first venture into WordPress and website design. This was my playground, where I learned to use WordPress, customize themes with CSS, and create a beautiful, functional site.

I love food in all forms - from cooking and eating it to thinking about sustainability, food culture, and nutrition. Mind Your Plants is a place for me to explore and write about food.

creating a blog

To create this blog, I spent a lot of time researching website essentials: how to buy a domain name, where to host the site, the difference between HTML and CSS, etc. I went into this blog as a complete beginner, with no knowledge of code or websites. Starting a blog from scratch taught me many things, and ignited the passion that has led me to becoming a website developer.

writing content

Writing content has been one of the most challenging aspects of having a blog, as I am an introvert and don’t naturally share my life with strangers. Just having this blog and updating it taught me to be a better writer and how to engage with an audience. The majority of the content on my blog follows my life and the recipes I share.

photography

One of the things I enjoy most is taking photos of the food I make. Just doing this blog has made me think about the lighting, composition, and colors in photos. Photos from my early recipes clearly show some beginner mistakes - poor lighting, yellow color cast, and uninteresting composition.
