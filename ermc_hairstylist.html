ermc hairstylist

wordpress // theme customization // content

Elizabeth McHenry is a hair stylist in Charlotte, NC. She's been trying to grow her business, but didn't have a website to give potential clients information about her and her services. Liz and I worked together to create a flexible website that allowed her to frequently add blog posts, images, and events. For these reasons, I decided to create the website on Wordpress, which would allow her the freedom to add and edit content without learning to code.

I worked with Liz throughout the process, from design to teaching her Wordpress, so that her website would reflect her design style and personality. This also ensured that the website has all of the features she needs to run her business, including integration with her social media and current branding.

initial design

Liz has a fun personality, so I worked with her closely during the design stage to ensure that her website's personality matches her own.

mobile-friendly and responsive

Liz's potential clients are likely to access her site from many devices, not just a desktop computer. In addition to choosing a responsive Wordpress theme, I intentionally made design choices that would look good on any device.

custom pages

Throughout the process of creating her site, Liz explained to me the types of information she wanted on her site. I added these specific pages for her, from events to gift certificates.

social media integration

Liz relies on social media to promote her business and find clients. This site is fully integrated with her social media, with footer links and social sharing on blog posts.
